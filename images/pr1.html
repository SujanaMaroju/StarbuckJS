Starbucks Reveals New Drinks For New Year's Eve

Gotta start the new year off right.

By Danielle JacksonPublished: Dec 27, 2017 9:00 AM EST

Christmas may be over, but that doesn't mean that Starbucks is done dominating the season with its ever-growing lineup of specialty drinks. With just a few days left of 2017, the coffee chain is already giving us something exciting to look forward to in the new year: the all-new Black and White Mocha Collection.

Starbucks is kicking off the start of 2018 with the debut of the collection, which will include a trio of black and white themed drinks — the Black and White Mocha, Black and White Hot Cocoa, and Black and White Frappuccino. According to a release, the Black and White Mocha starts off with Starbucks' signature espresso roast poured over a "silky swirl of white chocolate and dark mocha." Steamed milk is added to the drink before it's all topped off with whipped cream and chocolate "sequins." The Black and White Hot Chocolate combines dark mocha sauce, white chocolate mocha sauce, and steamed milk before also being topped with whipped cream and chocolate sequins. And lastly, the Black and White Frappuccino is essentially a frozen and caffeinated version of the aforementioned hot cocoa.

The Black and White Mocha collection is now on sale at participating Starbucks locations in the U.S. and Canada. All three beverages are available through the new year while supplies last.
